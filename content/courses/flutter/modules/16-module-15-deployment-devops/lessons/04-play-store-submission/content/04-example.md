Required and recommended visual assets:



```text
## Required Assets

### App Icon
- Size: 512 x 512 pixels
- Format: 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
- Must match the icon in your app
- No badges, "new" or "sale" overlays
- Background should reach edges (no rounded corners in source)

### Feature Graphic
- Size: 1024 x 500 pixels
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Displayed at top of store listing
- Should represent your app's brand/purpose
- Avoid text-heavy designs (doesn't translate well)
- Can include device mockups with app screenshots

### Screenshots (Phone)
- Minimum: 2 screenshots
- Maximum: 8 screenshots
- Size: Minimum 320px, maximum 3840px on any side
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 9:16 (portrait recommended for phones)
- Format: PNG or JPEG (no alpha)

### Screenshots (7-inch Tablet) - Optional
- Same requirements as phone
- Recommended if app has tablet-specific UI

### Screenshots (10-inch Tablet) - Optional
- Same requirements as phone
- Shows in Play Store on larger devices

## Optional Assets

### Video (Promo Video)
- YouTube URL (not unlisted or private)
- 30 seconds to 2 minutes recommended
- First 30 seconds most important
- No age-restricted content
```
