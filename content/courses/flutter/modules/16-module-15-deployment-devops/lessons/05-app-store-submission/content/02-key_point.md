**Creating Your App Record**

Before uploading your first build, create an app record in App Store Connect:

1. **Sign in to App Store Connect:** appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. **Click My Apps:** Then the "+" button
3. **Select New App:** Choose iOS platform

**Required Information:**

**Bundle ID:**
- Must match the bundle identifier in your Xcode project
- Format: com.company.appname
- Cannot be changed after submission
- Register in Apple Developer Portal first (Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles)

**SKU:**
- Stock Keeping Unit - your internal identifier
- Not visible to users
- Cannot be changed later
- Examples: MYAPP001, flutter-app-2024

**App Name:**
- Up to 30 characters
- Must be unique on the App Store
- Cannot include trademarked terms without authorization
- Avoid generic descriptions or keyword stuffing

**Primary Language:**
- Default language for your app listing
- Can add localizations later

**App Category:**
- Primary category determines where app appears in browse
- Optional secondary category for additional discovery
