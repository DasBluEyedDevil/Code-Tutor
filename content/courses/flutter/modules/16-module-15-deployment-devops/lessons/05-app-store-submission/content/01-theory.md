**App Store Connect Overview**

App Store Connect is Apple's portal for managing your iOS apps throughout their lifecycle. From here you can:
- Submit apps and updates for review
- Manage app metadata and pricing
- Distribute beta builds via TestFlight
- Access analytics and sales data
- Respond to customer reviews
- Configure in-app purchases and subscriptions

**Apple Developer Program Requirements**

Before submitting to the App Store, you need:

1. **Apple Developer Program Membership:** $99/year for individuals and organizations
2. **Valid Apple ID:** Used to sign in to App Store Connect
3. **Signed Build:** App must be signed with a distribution certificate
4. **App Record:** Created in App Store Connect before uploading

**Key Differences from Play Store:**

| Aspect | App Store | Play Store |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Annual Fee | $99/year | $25 one-time |
| Review Time | 24-48 hours (typically) | 1-7 days |
| Review Strictness | More thorough | Less strict |
| Beta Testing | TestFlight | Internal/Closed/Open tracks |
| Phased Rollout | 7-day automatic | Manual percentage |

**App Store Connect Roles:**

- **Account Holder:** Full access, manages legal agreements
- **Admin:** Manages apps, users, and agreements
- **App Manager:** Manages specific apps
- **Developer:** Uploads builds, views reports
- **Marketing:** Manages metadata and pricing
- **Sales:** Views financial reports
