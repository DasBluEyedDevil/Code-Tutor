### 4. Custom Backend (Node.js, Django, etc.)

**Pros**:
- ✅ Complete control
- ✅ No vendor lock-in
- ✅ Custom business logic

**Cons**:
- ❌ Must write and maintain server code
- ❌ Must handle scaling
- ❌ Must manage infrastructure
- ❌ Security is your responsibility

**Best for**: Advanced developers, specific requirements
