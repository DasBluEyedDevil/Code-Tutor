---


Let's recap:
- ✅ `if` lets programs make decisions
- ✅ `else` handles the "otherwise" case
- ✅ `else if` handles multiple options
- ✅ Comparison operators: `==`, `!=`, `>`, `<`, `>=`, `<=`
- ✅ Logical operators: `&&` (AND), `||` (OR), `!` (NOT)
- ✅ Conditions must evaluate to true or false
