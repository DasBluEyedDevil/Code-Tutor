**Google Play Review Process**

After submitting your app, Google reviews it before making it available:

**Review Timeline:**

- New apps: Usually 3-7 days (can be longer for new accounts)
- Updates to existing apps: Usually 1-3 days
- Internal testing track: No review (immediate)
- Expedited review: Available for critical updates (not guaranteed faster)

**What Google Reviews:**

1. **Policy Compliance:**
   - No malware, spyware, or deceptive behavior
   - Proper disclosure of permissions and data use
   - Appropriate content rating
   - No impersonation or misleading claims

2. **Functionality:**
   - App launches without crashing
   - Core features work as described
   - No broken links or dead ends

3. **Content:**
   - Store listing matches app content
   - No prohibited content (hate speech, violence, adult content)
   - Intellectual property compliance

4. **Privacy:**
   - Privacy policy present if required
   - Data safety section accurate
   - Permissions used appropriately
