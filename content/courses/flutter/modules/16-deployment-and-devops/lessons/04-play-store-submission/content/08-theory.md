**Understanding Release Tracks**

Google Play provides multiple release tracks to test your app before full production release:

**1. Internal Testing Track:**

- Up to 100 testers (email addresses you specify)
- App available within minutes of upload
- No review process - immediate availability
- Great for: Team testing, QA, stakeholder demos
- Testers must opt-in via link you share

**2. Closed Testing Track:**

- Invite-only testing with email lists or Google Groups
- Up to 10,000 testers per closed track (can have multiple tracks)
- Brief review before available (usually 1-3 days)
- Can require testers to be in specific countries
- Great for: Beta testers, external testing groups, phased testing

**3. Open Testing Track:**

- Anyone can join (no invite needed)
- Appears in Play Store with "Early Access" badge
- Full review process (same as production)
- Unlimited testers
- Users can find app in Play Store search
- Great for: Public beta, gathering feedback at scale

**4. Production Track:**

- Full public release
- Complete review process
- Appears in Play Store for all users
- Supports staged rollouts (percentage-based release)
