**IARC Content Rating System**

Google Play uses the International Age Rating Coalition (IARC) system to rate apps worldwide. A single questionnaire generates ratings for multiple regions:

| Region | Rating System |
|--------|---------------|
| Americas | ESRB (Entertainment Software Rating Board) |
| Europe | PEGI (Pan European Game Information) |
| Brazil | ClassInd |
| Germany | USK |
| Australia | ACB |
| Russia | RARS |

**Completing the Questionnaire:**

1. Navigate to: Policy -> App content -> Content ratings
2. Start new questionnaire for your app
3. Answer questions about your app's content honestly

**Content Categories Assessed:**

- Violence and physical harm
- Sexual content and nudity
- Language (profanity, crude humor)
- Controlled substances (drugs, alcohol, tobacco)
- Gambling and simulated gambling
- User-generated content and social features
- Location sharing and personal data collection
- In-app purchases
