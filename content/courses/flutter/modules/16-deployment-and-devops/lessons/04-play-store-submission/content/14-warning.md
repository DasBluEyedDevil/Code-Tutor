---


**Top Reasons Apps Get Rejected:**

1. **Metadata Issues:**
   - Keywords stuffed in title or description
   - Screenshots don't represent actual app
   - Misleading feature claims

2. **Policy Violations:**
   - Missing privacy policy when collecting data
   - Requesting unnecessary permissions
   - Deceptive ads or hidden costs

3. **Functionality Problems:**
   - App crashes on launch
   - Core features don't work
   - App requires account but signup is broken

4. **Content Rating Mismatch:**
   - App content more mature than rating indicates
   - User-generated content not properly moderated
   - Violence or language not disclosed in questionnaire

5. **Intellectual Property:**
   - Using trademarked names without permission
   - Copying other apps' designs or assets
   - Using copyrighted content without license

**If Your App is Rejected:**

1. Read rejection email carefully - identifies specific issues
2. Fix all mentioned issues (they may find new ones if you don't fix thoroughly)
3. Resubmit with explanation of changes made
4. Appeal if you believe rejection was incorrect (use appeal form)
5. Allow same review time for resubmission
