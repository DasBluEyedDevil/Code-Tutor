You can have multiple parameters:


**Output**:

**Order matters!** The values you pass must match the parameter order.



```dart
Hi! My name is Sarah.
I am 25 years old.
I live in New York.
Hi! My name is Mike.
I am 30 years old.
I live in Los Angeles.
```
