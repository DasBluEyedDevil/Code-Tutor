---


**AnimatedContainer** is the most versatile implicit animation widget. It can animate almost any visual property:

- **Size**: width, height
- **Color**: color, decoration colors
- **Position**: margin, padding, alignment
- **Shape**: borderRadius, boxShadow
- **Transform**: rotation, scale (via transform)
