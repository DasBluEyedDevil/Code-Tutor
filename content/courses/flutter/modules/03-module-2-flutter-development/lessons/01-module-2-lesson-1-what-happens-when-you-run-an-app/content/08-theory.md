title: "The Widget Tree (Simplified)"
---


Look at how our widgets are nested:


This is called a **widget tree**. Each widget can have children (widgets inside it), creating a tree structure.

Think of it like:
- **MaterialApp** is the trunk
- **Center** is a branch
- **Text** is a leaf



```dart
MaterialApp
  └─ Center
      └─ Text
```
