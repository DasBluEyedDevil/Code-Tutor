title: "Introducing TextStyle"
---


To style text, we use the `style` property with a `TextStyle`:


**Conceptual**: Think of `TextStyle` as the formatting toolbar in Word or Google Docs.



```dart
Text(
  'Hello, Flutter!',
  style: TextStyle(
    fontSize: 24,
    color: Colors.blue,
  ),
)
```
