Deploying your React app is the same:
- Move from localhost to real internet
- Build optimized version (Vite build)
- Get a public URL (vercel.app or netlify.app)
- Hosted on fast servers worldwide (CDN)
- Connects to your deployed API!

Popular platforms: Vercel (made by Next.js creators, free!), Netlify, Cloudflare Pages
