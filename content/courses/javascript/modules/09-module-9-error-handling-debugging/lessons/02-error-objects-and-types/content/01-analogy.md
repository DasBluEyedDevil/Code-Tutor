title: "The Dashboard Warning Lights"
---

Imagine you're driving a modern car. When something goes wrong, you don't just see a generic "Help" light. You get specific icons:

*   **The Gas Pump:** You're out of fuel.
*   **The Thermometer:** Your engine is overheating.
*   **The Tire:** Your tire pressure is low.

Each light tells you exactly **where** the problem is so you can fix it. If the gas light is on, you go to a gas station. If the engine light is on, you pull over and call a tow truck.
