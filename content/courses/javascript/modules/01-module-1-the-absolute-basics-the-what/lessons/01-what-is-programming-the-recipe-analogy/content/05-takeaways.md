type: "TAKEAWAY"
title: "Key Takeaways"
---

*   **Programming** is giving specific instructions to a computer.
*   **JavaScript** is the language we are using to communicate.
*   `console.log()` is used to print text or numbers to the screen.
*   **Strings** (text) must be wrapped in quotes (`'...'` or `"..."`).
*   **Statements** should end with a semicolon (`;`).
