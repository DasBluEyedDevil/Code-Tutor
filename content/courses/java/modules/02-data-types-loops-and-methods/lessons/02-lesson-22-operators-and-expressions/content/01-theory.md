title: "The Problem: Doing Math with Variables"
---

You can store numbers in variables, but programs need to DO THINGS with those numbers:

• A calculator adds, subtracts, multiplies, divides
• A game tracks score: score = score + 10
• A store calculates total: price * quantity
• A thermometer converts: fahrenheit = (celsius * 9/5) + 32

Java provides OPERATORS—symbols that perform operations on values.

Think of operators as the buttons on a calculator: +, -, ×, ÷
