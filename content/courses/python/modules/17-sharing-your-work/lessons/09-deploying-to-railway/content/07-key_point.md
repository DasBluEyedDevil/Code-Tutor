title: "Key Takeaways"
---

- **Railway CLI** (`railway up`) is the fastest deployment method
- **`railway add --database postgresql`** provisions database instantly
- **railway.toml** customizes build and deploy settings
- **PORT environment variable** is set by Railway - don't hardcode ports
- **`railway run`** executes commands in the Railway environment
- **Automatic HTTPS** and custom domains included
- **Usage-based pricing** is predictable but monitor your credits
