**Project architecture = Blueprint for your code**

**Think of building a house:**

**Without planning:**
- Start building walls randomly
- Forget about plumbing
- Add rooms wherever
- Result: Unusable mess

**With planning:**
- Draw blueprints first
- Plan room layout
- Consider utilities (water, electricity)
- Result: Livable, maintainable home
