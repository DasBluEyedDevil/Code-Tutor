title: "Key Takeaways"
---

- **render.yaml** defines your entire infrastructure as code
- **Free tier** is great for learning but has cold starts and time limits
- **`fromDatabase`** automatically links services to databases
- **`generateValue: true`** creates secure random values for secrets
- **Health checks** are essential for Render to know your app is running
- **Auto-deploy** triggers on every push to your connected branch
- Convert **DATABASE_URL** format for async SQLAlchemy compatibility
