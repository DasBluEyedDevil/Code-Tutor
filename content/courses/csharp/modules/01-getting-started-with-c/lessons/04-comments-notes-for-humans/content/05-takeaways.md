type: "KEY_POINT"
title: "Key Takeaways"
---

- **Comments** are notes for humans that the computer ignores.
- **`//`** starts a single-line comment.
- **`/* ... */`** creates a multi-line comment.
- **Comment the WHY**, not the WHAT (code already shows the what).
- **Keep comments updated** when you change code.
- **`///`** (XML comments) are used for professional documentation.
