type: "KEY_POINT"
title: "Key Takeaways"
---

- **Programming** is giving precise, step-by-step instructions to a computer.
- **C#** is a powerful, precise, and statically typed language.
- **`Console.WriteLine()`** displays text to the screen.
- **Comments** (`//`) are notes for humans that the computer ignores.
- **Semicolons** (`;`) are required at the end of every instruction.
- **Text** (Strings) must be enclosed in double quotes (`"Hello"`).
- **Case Sensitivity** means `Console` and `console` are different.
