Kotlin 2.0 is a major milestone that brings significant improvements to the language and compiler. Released in May 2024, this version introduces the new K2 compiler as the default, delivering faster compilation times and better IDE support.

In this lesson, you'll learn:
- What the K2 compiler is and why it matters
- Key improvements in Kotlin 2.0
- Smarter smart casts and type inference
- Migration considerations

Understanding these improvements will help you write better Kotlin code and take advantage of the latest features!
