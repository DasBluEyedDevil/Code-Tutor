### Key Takeaways

1. **K2 is a complete rewrite** of the Kotlin compiler frontend
2. **2x faster compilation** in many projects
3. **Smarter type inference** handles complex cases better
4. **Better smart casts** through complex control flow
5. **Clearer error messages** with actionable suggestions
6. **Unified architecture** benefits both compiler and IDE

### When to Adopt K2

- **New projects**: Start with K2 immediately
- **Existing projects**: Test with K2, migrate when dependencies support it
- **Library authors**: Ensure your libraries work with K2
