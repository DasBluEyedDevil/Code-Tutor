Kotlin 2.0 introduces the K2 compiler, a complete rewrite of the Kotlin compiler frontend. This represents the most significant compiler change in Kotlin's history.

In this lesson, you'll learn:
- What the K2 compiler is and why JetBrains rewrote it
- The performance improvements you can expect
- New language features enabled by K2
- How to prepare your projects for K2
