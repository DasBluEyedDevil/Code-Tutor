### Major Libraries with KSP Support

**Android/JVM**
- Room 2.4+
- Moshi 1.13+
- Dagger/Hilt 2.48+
- Koin 4.x (annotations via KSP)
- Glide 4.14+
- Lyricist (i18n)
- Ktorfit

**Multiplatform**
- kotlinx.serialization (built-in, no KSP needed)
- SQLDelight
- Apollo GraphQL
- Koin Annotations

**Still Require kapt**
- Some older libraries
- Custom annotation processors not yet migrated
- MapStruct (migration in progress)
