### What You Need

1. **Google Play Developer Account** ($25 one-time fee)
   - Register at [play.google.com/console](https://play.google.com/console)
   
2. **App Bundle** (AAB file)
   - Signed with your upload key
   - Generated with `./gradlew bundleRelease`

3. **Store Listing Assets**
   - App icon (512x512 PNG)
   - Feature graphic (1024x500 PNG)
   - Screenshots (min 2, various sizes)
   - Short description (80 chars max)
   - Full description (4000 chars max)
   - Privacy policy URL

4. **Content Rating Questionnaire**
   - Completed in Play Console

5. **Target API Level**
   - Must meet Google's current requirements (API 34+ as of 2025)
