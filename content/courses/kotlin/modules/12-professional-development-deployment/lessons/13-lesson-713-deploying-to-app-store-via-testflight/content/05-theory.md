### Internal Testing

- Up to 100 testers
- Must be App Store Connect users
- No review required
- Instant access after upload

### External Testing

- Up to 10,000 testers
- Anyone with email
- Requires Beta App Review (usually 24-48 hours)
- Public link available

### Setting Up TestFlight

1. Upload your build (Xcode Organizer or xcrun altool)
2. Wait for processing (~10-30 minutes)

4. Select your build
5. Add test information (what to test, feedback email)

   - External: Create group, add emails or public link
