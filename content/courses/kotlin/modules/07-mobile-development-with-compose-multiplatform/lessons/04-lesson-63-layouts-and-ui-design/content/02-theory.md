Beautiful UI is crucial for app success. Users judge apps within **milliseconds** - if your UI looks outdated or confusing, users uninstall. With Compose Multiplatform, you can create stunning UIs that work on both Android and iOS.

In this lesson, you'll master:
- ✅ Advanced layout composables (Box, LazyColumn, LazyRow)
- ✅ Arrangement and alignment strategies
- ✅ Spacer for spacing control
- ✅ Material Design 3 components
- ✅ Theming: colors, typography, shapes
- ✅ Building complex, professional UIs
- ✅ Platform-specific considerations for Android and iOS
