**How it works:**
1. `for` - Keyword that starts the loop
2. `i` - Loop variable (can be any name)
3. `in` - Keyword meaning "within" or "through"
4. `1..5` - Range from 1 to 5 (inclusive)
5. Loop body executes once for each value in the range
